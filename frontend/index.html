Sample protocol — read this first

  1. Mix turmeric in isopropyl alcohol, let it settle 5 min.
  2. Take the CLEAR coloured liquid from the top — leave the powder behind.
  3. Put a thin, even layer of this liquid (ideally in a small clear glass) on/over white paper.
  4. Photo 1 = liquid only.
  5. Add 1 drop FeCl₃, mix, then Photo 2.
Do NOT photograph the wet paste or solid blob — it saturates the reading and pure samples will read the same as adulterated ones.

Same white background, same light, same distance, and locked phone exposure for both photos.
